About Me

I’m Marium, a passionate and detail-oriented full-stack web developer with hands-on experience in both frontend and backend technologies. I specialize in building fully responsive, mobile-friendly websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, and Bootstrap, ensuring smooth user experiences across all devices. On the backend, I'm proficient in PHP and MySQL, with a solid understanding of database design, form handling, and dynamic content management. I also have extensive experience working with WordPress, including theme customization, plugin integration, and building client-ready websites using popular themes and page builders like Elementor, WPBakery, Gutenberg, and Divi. I’ve worked with themes such as Avada, Astra, Woodmart, Magbook, and BeTheme, adapting them to suit different industries and design needs. I take pride in writing clean, maintainable code and continuously learning new technologies to stay up-to-date with industry trends. My approach combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving, allowing me to deliver websites that are not only functional but also visually engaging. I'm confident working both independently and as part of a collaborative team, always focused on delivering high-quality results on time.
